System Requirements Specification (SRS)

 

New Labor Status Form Interface

  1. New Labor Status Form interface must allow Admin to create:
    •    A primary labor status form for a student
    •    A secondary labor status form for a student who already has an approved primary job
    •    A labor status form for a break term
    •    A primary overload form  
    •    Multiple labor status forms for the different students with the same Labor Supervisor and and Department in the same term.
