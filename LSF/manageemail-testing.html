Manage Email Template Interface Testing Scenarios

Testing Steps:

On the Manage Email Template Page fill in the following information:

  1. Recipient: Student
  2. Form Type: Labor Status Form
  3. Action: Submitted . This prompts the subject to change to "Labor Status Form Received"
  4. Edit the body
  5. A modal pops up and ask "Are you sure you want to save?" Click Submit.
Expected Outcome:
  1. There's a green flash at the top of the page that says that the Email Template "Student Labor Status Form Submitted" has successfully been changed.
  2. The page refreshes to a new mange email template page.
Verification:

On the Manage Email Template Page fill in the following information:

  1. Recipient: Student
  2. Form Type: Labor Status Form
  3. Action: Submitted . This prompts the subject to change to "Labor Status Form Received"
  4. In the body, the changes you've made is there now.
