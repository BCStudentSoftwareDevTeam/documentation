Labor Status Forms - System Requirements Specification (SRS)

New Labor Status Form Interface 

  1. The Labor Administrator must be able to:
    • Hire a student into a primary labor position
    • Hire a student into primary overload position
    • Hire a student into a secondary labor position
    • Hire a student into a secondary overload position
    • Hire students for the same department in the same term at the same time
    • Hire a student into a position with WLS greater than 5
    • Leave a note for a student prior to submission of a Labor Status Form

 

Administration > Pending Forms Interface

Pending Labor Status Forms 

1. The Labor Administrator must be able to:

  • Approve a pending Labor Status Form
  • Approve multiple pending Labor Status Forms
  • Deny a pending Labor Status Form
  • Modify a pending Labor Status Form
  • View notes tied to a pending Labor Status Form

Pending Adjustment Forms

1. The Labor Administartor must be able to:

  • Approve a pending Labor Adjustment Form
  • Approve multiple pending Labor Adjustment Forms
  • Deny a pending Labor Adjustment Form
  • Modify a pending Labor Adjustment Form
  • View notes tied to a pending Labor Adjustment Form

Pending Overload Forms

1. The Labor Administrator must be able to:

  • Approve a pending overload form
  • Approve a pending overload form reluctantly
  • Deny a pending overload form
  • Send email to SAAS and Financial Aid
  • Modify a pending overload form
  • View all the notes tied to a pending overload form

Pending Release Forms

1. The Labor Administrator must be able to:

  • Approve a pending release form
  • Deny a pending release form
  • View all the notes tied to a pending release form

Administration > Manage Terms

1. The Labor Administrator must be able to:

  • Open a term
  • Close a term
  • Set a term's start and end dates
  • Set a due date for the primary labor status forms
  • Set a due date for the adjustment forms
